## H1
Emergency Care at Tustin Village Animal Hospital

## Section: Body

In a pet emergency, every second counts. At Tustin Village Animal Hospital, our experienced team is ready to provide fast, life-saving care for your pet when they need it most.

Accidents and sudden illnesses can happen at any time. Our emergency care services are designed to provide urgent medical attention for pets experiencing severe health issues, trauma, or distress.

Our hospital is equipped with advanced emergency equipment and staffed by skilled veterinarians who are trained to handle critical cases with speed and expertise.

## Emergency services offered

- **Trauma & injury treatment** — Immediate care for accidents, fractures, and wounds.
- **Severe illness management** — Urgent treatment for seizures, poisoning, and difficulty breathing.
- **Emergency surgery** — Life-saving surgical procedures for critical conditions.
- **Shock & cardiac arrest response** — Rapid intervention for heart-related emergencies.
- **Heatstroke & dehydration treatment** — Care for pets affected by extreme weather.

## FAQs

**Q: How do I know if my pet needs emergency care?**
A: Seek immediate care if your pet is experiencing severe breathing difficulties, excessive bleeding, sudden collapse, seizures, or poisoning.

**Q: Can I bring my pet in without an appointment?**
A: Yes, emergencies are prioritized. Calling ahead allows us to prepare for your arrival.

**Q: What should I do before coming in?**
A: Remain calm, safely transport your pet, and contact us for first-aid guidance.
