## H1
Pet Travel at Tustin Village Animal Hospital

## Section: Body

Traveling with a pet requires careful planning and regulatory compliance. Our veterinary team offers complete pet travel consultations to help meet airline, domestic, and international travel requirements, ensuring pets are healthy, comfortable, and fully prepared for their journey.

## Services provided

- **International & domestic health certificates** — USDA-approved health certificates for hassle-free travel.
- **Rabies vaccination & other required vaccines** — Up-to-date vaccinations based on destination requirements.
- **Microchipping for identification** — Secure identification to prevent lost pets during travel.
- **Sedation & anxiety management** — Safe solutions for pets who experience travel anxiety.
- **Airline & destination-specific requirements** — Guidance on pet-friendly travel policies and documentation.

## FAQs

**Q: What documents do I need for my pet to travel?**
A: Most airlines and countries require a health certificate from a licensed veterinarian, proof of vaccinations, and microchip information.

**Q: How soon before my trip should I schedule a pet travel exam?**
A: We recommend scheduling your pet's travel consultation at least 30 days in advance to meet all necessary requirements.

**Q: Can my pet travel in the cabin with me?**
A: This depends on airline policies and your pet's size; we can guide you on optimal travel options based on individual needs.

**Q: What can I do if my pet experiences travel anxiety?**
A: We offer safe sedation options and travel-friendly solutions to keep pets calm and comfortable during transit.
