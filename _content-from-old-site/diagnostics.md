Diagnostic Services at Tustin Village Animal Hospital

## Section: Body

At Tustin Village Animal Hospital, our skilled veterinary team utilizes cutting-edge diagnostic tools, including digital imaging, laboratory testing, and ultrasound, to assess pet conditions with accuracy and efficiency. Whether your pet is experiencing unexplained symptoms or requires routine health screenings, we provide thorough evaluations.

Early detection is key to maintaining your pet's long-term health.

## Diagnostic services offered

- **Digital X-Rays** — High-resolution imaging for accurate internal assessments.
- **Ultrasound imaging** — Non-invasive scans to evaluate soft tissues and organ function.
- **Comprehensive bloodwork** — Early detection of infections, organ diseases, and metabolic disorders.
- **Urinalysis & fecal testing** — Identifies infections, parasites, and digestive conditions.
- **Electrocardiograms (ECG)** — Monitors heart health and detects irregularities.

## FAQs

**Q: When does my pet need diagnostic testing?**
A: Diagnostics are recommended for pets showing signs of illness, during annual checkups, or before surgeries to ensure they are in good health.

**Q: Are X-rays and ultrasounds safe for pets?**
A: Both procedures are non-invasive and completely safe, using modern equipment that minimizes exposure and maximizes accuracy.

**Q: How quickly will I get test results?**
A: Many results are available the same day, while complex diagnostics may take several days. Our team communicates results promptly.
