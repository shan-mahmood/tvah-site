At Tustin Village Animal Hospital, we believe in proactive dental care to prevent issues before they develop. Poor oral hygiene can lead to infections, tooth loss, and even organ damage. Our veterinary team provides professional cleanings, advanced procedures, and customized at-home care plans.

## FAQs

**Q: How often should my pet have a dental exam?**
A: Pets should have wellness exams at least yearly. Regular checkups help monitor health, catch issues early, and maintain vaccinations. Older pets or those with health concerns may need more frequent visits.

**Q: How can I tell if my pet has dental disease?**
A: Signs include bad breath, excessive drooling, difficulty eating, inflamed gums, and loose teeth. Schedule a dental exam promptly if you notice these symptoms.

**Q: How can I maintain my pet's dental health at home?**
A: Regular tooth brushing, dental treats, and vet-approved dental products support oral health between professional cleanings.
