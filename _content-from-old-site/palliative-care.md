## H1
Palliative Care at Tustin Village Animal Hospital

## Section: Body

At Tustin Village Animal Hospital, we provide compassionate palliative care to ensure your pet's comfort and dignity during chronic illness or advanced age. Our goal is to improve their quality of life while offering support and guidance for pet owners.

Pets facing long-term medical conditions receive personalized care plans developed collaboratively between our veterinary team and families. Services range from pain relief to mobility support, maintaining quality of life while keeping owners informed throughout treatment.

## Services offered

- **Pain management** — Medication and therapies to ease discomfort from arthritis, cancer, or other chronic illnesses.
- **Nutritional support** — Tailored diets and supplements to maintain weight and overall health.
- **Mobility assistance** — Adaptive equipment and physical therapy to help pets move comfortably.
- **Emotional & behavioral support** — Guidance on stress relief and lifestyle enrichment.
- **Hospice & end-of-life planning** — Compassionate discussions to help families make informed decisions.

## FAQs

**Q: How do I know if my pet needs palliative care?**
A: Pets experiencing chronic pain, mobility issues, or difficulty eating may benefit from palliative treatments.

**Q: Can palliative care improve my pet's quality of life?**
A: Yes — it focuses on maintaining comfort and well-being, allowing pets to enjoy remaining time with reduced pain.

**Q: When should I consider hospice care?**
A: Hospice is recommended when a pet's condition is no longer treatable, shifting focus to comfort and emotional support.
