## Four featured services (verbatim)

### Internal Medicine
Our veterinarians are equipped to manage a wide range of illnesses in dogs and cats, regardless of breed, size, or life stage. This includes diagnosing and treating both acute and chronic conditions, as well as promoting health and preventing disease.

### Wellness Exams
We prioritize proactive disease prevention over a "wait and see" approach. We offer comprehensive exams for both general wellness and sick pets. During each exam, our veterinarians provide a thorough evaluation, and make specific recommendations based on your pet's individual needs.

### Palliative Care
At Tustin Village Animal Hospital, we prioritize the quality of life for our patients. Our approach to veterinary palliative medicine involves declining or withdrawing curative therapies for life-limiting illnesses to ensure that patients do not suffer needlessly. The focus shifts to providing comfort and care as a dog or cat nears the end of life.

### Surgery
As a pet owner, you may face important decisions about your pet's health, including surgery. While surgery might seem daunting, it can prevent serious health issues, restore function, and even save your pet's life. Tustin Village's Animal Hospital provides a wide range of veterinary surgery treatment options to ensure the best care for your pet.
