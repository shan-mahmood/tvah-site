## Section: Comprehensive Wellness Care for Your Pet

At Tustin Village Animal Hospital, our thorough wellness examinations go beyond just a basic checkup. Our experienced veterinary team performs detailed assessments covering all aspects of pet health. We recognize each pet is unique and create personalized health plans tailored to their specific needs, age, lifestyle, and health history. From energetic puppies needing vaccination support to specialized preventative care for senior cats, we provide care across every life stage.

- **Senior pet wellness screening** — Advanced monitoring for age-related conditions.
- **Behavioral consultation** — Expert guidance for training and behavior modification.
- **Vaccination assessment and updates** — Customized vaccination schedules based on lifestyle and risk factors.

## FAQs

**Q: How often should my pet have a wellness exam?**
A: Pets should have a wellness exam at least once a year, but older pets or those with chronic health conditions may need more frequent visits.

**Q: What can I expect during a wellness exam?**
A: During the exam, your pet will undergo a full physical check-up. The veterinarian will discuss any concerns, implement preventative measures, and offer guidance on maintaining optimal pet health.

**Q: Do I need to prepare my pet for the exam?**
A: No special preparation is needed. Bring your pet on their regular schedule and be ready to share any observed behavioral or health changes.
