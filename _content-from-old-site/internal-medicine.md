## Section: Comprehensive Internal Medicine Care for Your Pet

Internal medicine encompasses a wide range of medical conditions affecting pet organs and bodily functions. Our veterinarians utilize advanced diagnostic tools and personalized treatment plans to manage and treat illnesses effectively. Whether your pet is experiencing digestive issues, respiratory problems, or chronic diseases, expert medical support is available.

We take a comprehensive approach to internal medicine, combining cutting-edge technology with compassionate care to help pets achieve optimal health.

## Services we provide

- **Endocrine disorders management** — Diagnosis and treatment of diabetes, thyroid disorders, and adrenal gland diseases.
- **Gastrointestinal disorders** — Management of chronic vomiting, diarrhea, and inflammatory bowel disease (IBD).
- **Respiratory disease treatment** — Care for asthma, pneumonia, and breathing difficulties.
- **Kidney & liver disease management** — Specialized treatments to improve organ function and quality of life.
- **Infectious disease control** — Diagnosis and treatment of bacterial, viral, and fungal infections.
- **Autoimmune & immune-mediated diseases** — Advanced therapies for conditions like lupus and immune-mediated anemia.

## FAQs

**Q: What are the signs that my pet may have an internal health issue?**
A: Common symptoms include weight loss, lethargy, vomiting, diarrhea, excessive drinking or urination, and difficulty breathing. Schedule an exam immediately if you notice these signs.

**Q: How are internal medical conditions diagnosed?**
A: We use a combination of blood tests, imaging (X-rays, ultrasound), urinalysis, and other diagnostic tools to assess your pet's condition accurately.

**Q: Can internal diseases be cured?**
A: Some conditions can be managed effectively with medication and lifestyle changes, while others require ongoing treatment. We create personalized plans to optimize quality of life.
