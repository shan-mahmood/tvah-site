## H1
Telemedicine at Tustin Village Animal Hospital

## Section: Body

At Tustin Village Animal Hospital, we understand that busy schedules and pet anxiety can make in-person visits challenging. Our telemedicine services provide expert veterinary advice and support without the stress of travel. Whether you need guidance on a health concern or a follow-up consultation, we are here to offer quality care — anytime, anywhere.

## Services offered

- **Virtual consultations** — Discuss minor illnesses, skin conditions, or behavioral concerns directly with a veterinarian.
- **Medication & prescription refills** — Obtain prescription renewals and dosage adjustments conveniently.
- **Post-surgical & follow-up care** — Monitor recovery progress without unnecessary travel.
- **Nutritional & wellness advice** — Receive guidance on diet, supplements, and preventive care.

## FAQs

**Q: How do I schedule a telemedicine appointment?**
A: Direct contact with our office is required; online booking is unavailable. Staff verify eligibility and address initial questions before scheduling.

**Q: What is the cost?**
A: Thirty-minute consultations cost $60, providing professional guidance from home.

**Q: Who is eligible?**
A: Only existing patients seen within the last 12 months qualify. Pets not visited in a year require initial in-person appointments.

**Q: Are emergency cases covered?**
A: No — telemedicine handles non-emergency cases only. Medical emergencies require immediate in-person care.
